About Me

Where medicine meets intelligence

I'm a third-year Biomedical Informatics student with a deep interest in how artificial intelligence can transform healthcare — from speeding up diagnosis to making clinical decision-making more reliable and equitable.

My work spans machine learning, deep learning, and clinical NLP. I enjoy building things from scratch, understanding why models work (or don't), and thinking critically about the ethics of AI in high-stakes environments.

Outside of projects, I'm an active member of the ACM Student Chapter and always looking for ways to connect with people working at the frontier of health technology.
